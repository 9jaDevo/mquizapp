We love what you’re doing and believe the mQuiz mobile app can make Trivia Thursday even smoother and fairer. Below is a short proposal outlining what we can offer and how it helps.

## What mQuiz does for Trivia Thursday

- Instant scoring and leaderboards: participants answer in the app and scores are calculated automatically in real time. No manual tallying or message-thread confusion.
- Consistent UX: questions, timers, and answer input are unified — participants don’t need to scroll the chat to follow instructions or watch for messages.
- Fair play & bot-detection: our platform has measures to reduce automated/bot responses and discourage unfair answering methods, helping protect the spirit of live competition.
- Analytics and reporting: track participation, accuracy, top performers, and engagement — useful for announcing winners and improving future quizzes.
- Frictionless prize distribution: with clear results and leaderboards it’s easier to award prizes promptly.

## Why this helps GDG Abuja

- Saves volunteer time: organizers won’t need to collect, collate, and verify answers from chat messages.
- Better participant experience: faster rounds, clearer rules, and a clean leaderboard increase excitement and repeat participation.
- Reduces cheating: while no system is perfect, using a dedicated quiz app raises the bar for fairness compared to open chat answers.
