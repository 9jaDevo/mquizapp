### 1. Daily Streaks ✅
Users earn coins for consecutive logins:
- Configurable daily reward (default: 10 coins)
- Milestone bonuses (default: 50 coins every 7 days)
- Leaderboard tracking
- Admin dashboard with statistics

### 2. Device Tracking ✅
Prevents multi-account fraud:
- Device fingerprinting via device_id
- One account per device enforcement
- Automatic suspension on violation
- Admin management interface

### 3. Fraud Detection ✅
3-layer protection system:
- Ad spam limit (default: 100/day)
- Quiz cheating detection (speed/accuracy combo)
- New account withdrawal lock (default: 7 days)
- Admin review queue with resolution workflow

### 4. Sponsor Banners ✅
Rotating sponsor advertisements:
- Date-based activation
- Impression limits (daily/weekly/monthly)
- Priority-based rotation
- Analytics with CTR, unique users, clicks
- Image upload management

### 5. Payout Eligibility ✅
Real user validation:
- Minimum active days requirement (default: 20 in 30)
- Configurable lookback window
- User-friendly messages
- Prevents bot account payouts

### 6. Boost Earnings ✅
Double-coin incentive:
- Show offer after quiz
- 2x coin multiplier (configurable)
- Requires ad watch (optional)
- Instant coin crediting

### 7. Watch & Unlock ✅
