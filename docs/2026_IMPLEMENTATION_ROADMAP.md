## 🚧 Risk Management

### Identified Risks

#### Technical Risks
**Risk**: AI models underperform  
**Mitigation**: Extensive testing, gradual rollout, fallback mechanisms  
**Impact**: Medium | Likelihood: Low  

**Risk**: Performance issues at scale  
**Mitigation**: Load testing, scalable infrastructure, monitoring  
**Impact**: High | Likelihood: Medium  

#### Market Risks
**Risk**: Increased competition  
**Mitigation**: Differentiation through AI, community focus  
**Impact**: Medium | Likelihood: High  

**Risk**: ASO algorithm changes  
**Mitigation**: Diversified acquisition, strong organic growth  
**Impact**: Medium | Likelihood: Medium  

#### Resource Risks
**Risk**: Development delays  
**Mitigation**: Agile methodology, buffer time, MVP approach  
**Impact**: Medium | Likelihood: Medium  

**Risk**: Budget overruns  
**Mitigation**: Phased rollout, strict budget tracking, ROI monitoring  
**Impact**: High | Likelihood: Low
